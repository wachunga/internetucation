Web browsers can be downloaded at the company site (Netscape or Microsoft) or at any popular software site such as Tucows.

To change the display properties of the monitors, you must have access to the computers' control panels. In the control panel, under "Display", is tab entitled "Settings" on the far right. From there both the colour palette and desktop area (screen resolution) can be changed appropriately (to 16 bit and 800 x 600, respectively). It is entirely possible that the control panels are not accessible without the proper password. If so, it may be necessary to contact the school technician (person in charge of computers).

To check the version of your browser, open the menu entitled "Help" on the toolbar and click on "About Internet Explorer" or "About Navigator...", depending, of course, on which browser you are using. If the version number is less than 3.x, it is recommended that you upgrade to a newer version.
