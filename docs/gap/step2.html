To analyse a poem, it is a good habit to read it several times. If the poem is short to medium in length, you are recommended to read it at least three times, aloud if possible.

During the first reading, which you are about to do, focus only on the general content of the poem. This includes identifying the speaker, audience, setting, characters (if any), as well as the plot or action.

After reading through the poem for the first time, briefly paraphrase what you have read (in this case, using the text box provided below).
