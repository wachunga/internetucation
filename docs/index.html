This site is an experimental project designed to be a free resource for teachers. Internetucation designs custom online lessons specifically for class presentation in school computer labs, based on suggestions by teachers and students worldwide.

Additional information, including the project's background and objective, is located on the information page. To take advantage of any of the available lessons, you must first register. If, on the other hand, you are interested in submitting a suggestion, please visit the suggestions section of the site.
